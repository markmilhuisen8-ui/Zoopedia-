SOS Radar.
Built for the wild.

Our most advanced safety tool yet. SOS Radar provides real-time emergency tracking and survival guides that work entirely offline when every second counts.

1
Activate: Simply tap the SOS icon in the nav or shake your device.
2
Locate: The radar locks your GPS and shows nearby rescue points.
3
Signal: High-frequency pings are broadcast to any nearby device.
