Getting Started

How to Use
Zoopedia

Explore the wild like never before. Follow these simple steps to dive into your animal adventure.

1
Start Here

Create your account

Sign up in seconds with your email. Your profile unlocks personalized animal recommendations, saved species, and your full Zoopedia history.

🦁

Free to join

No subscription needed. Full access to the animal library from day one.

2
Discover

Browse or search animals

Use the search bar or explore by habitat, diet, or category. Each animal card surfaces rich facts, sound clips, and stunning photography.

🔍

Smart search

Search by name, habitat, or even a fun fact — Zoopedia finds it instantly.

3
Interact

Go hands-free with AI tracking

Enable your camera and let Zoopedia's hand-tracking AI take over. Navigate menus, flip cards, and interact with 3D models — no touch required.

✋

Gesture controls

Wave, pinch, or point — the AI responds to your natural movements in real time.

4
Emergency

Use SOS Radar when you need it

Lost in the wild? Open the SOS Radar from any screen. It works offline too — detecting your location and broadcasting an emergency signal instantly.

🆘

Always available

SOS works even without internet. Your safety is always the first priority.

5
Enjoy

Save favourites & share

Star any animal to save it to your personal collection. Share species cards with friends or export them for school projects and research.

Ready to explore?

The entire animal kingdom is one tap away.
