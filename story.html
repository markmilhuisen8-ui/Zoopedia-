Animal Vault

Chapter I

THE CROWN OF GALE

As the golden sun rises over the African savanna, life awakens across the Pride Lands. Animals from every corner gather beneath the towering Pride Rock. At its peak stands the mighty king, Mufasa. Beside him, a newborn cub is presented to the world. His name is Simba. And as he is lifted high, the kingdom bows… welcoming its future king.

Chapter II

THE GOLDEN MANE

Simba grows into a playful and curious cub, full of excitement about becoming king one day. His father teaches him about the balance of life. how every creature has a place in the great Circle. But far from the sunlight, hidden in darkness,one lion watches with envy. Scar.

Chapter III

Chapter IV

Driven by curiosity, Simba explores beyond his limits with his friend Nala. They wander into the forbidden Elephant Graveyard. where danger lurks. Suddenly, hyenas surround them, closing in. Fear rises. until a powerful roar shakes the ground.

Chapter IV

THE GORGE'S SILENCE

Mufasa arrives just in time, saving Simba and Nala from danger. But far away, Scar sets a darker plan in motion. He lures Simba into a narrow gorge. and triggers a massive stampede.Mufasa rushes in, rescuing his son in a heroic act… but as he climbs to safety, Scar betrays him. The king falls. and the Pride Lands lose their ruler.

Chapter V

A KING WITHOUT A NAME

Heartbroken, Simba is manipulated into believing the tragedy is his fault. He runs away… leaving behind his home and identity. Lost in the wild, he is rescued by Timon and Pumbaa, who teach him a carefree life Hakuna Matata. Years pass, and Simba grows into adulthood. forgetting who he truly is.

Chapter VI

THE KING'S LONG SHADOW

Meanwhile, under Scar’s rule, the Pride Lands fall into ruin. Food becomes scarce, and hope begins to fade. When Nala finds Simba alive, she urges him to return but he refuses, trapped by guilt. Only after a powerful reminder from Rafiki. and the voice of his father in the stars… does Simba find the courage to go back and face his past.

Chapter VII

RAIN ON THE SCORCHED EARTH

Simba returns to a broken kingdom and confronts Scar. The truth is revealed… and a fierce battle begins. In the end, Simba defeats Scar and reclaims his rightful place as king. Rain falls, restoring life to the land. And as time passes, a new cub is lifted at Pride Rock.The Circle of Life continues… forever.

Alex Sussi
